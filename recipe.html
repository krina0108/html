Classic Masala Maggi Recipe (Serves 1-2)

Ingredients:

  • 1 packet Maggi instant noodles
  • 1½ cups water
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped) – optional
  • 1 small tomato (chopped) – optional
  • ½ tsp oil or butter
  • 1 green chili (optional, chopped)
  • 1 pinch turmeric (optional)
  • Vegetables like peas, carrots, or capsicum (optional)

Steps:

  1. Heat the pan

    2. Add ½ tsp oil or butter to a pan and heat on medium flame.

  2. Sauté veggies (optional)

    3. Add chopped onions, green chili, and other vegetables. Cook for 2–3 minutes until soft. Add a pinch of turmeric if using.

  3. Add water

    4. Pour 1½ cups of water into the pan. Bring it to a boil.

  4. Add Maggi noodles

    5. Break the noodle cake into 4 parts and add it to the boiling water.

  5. Add taste-maker

    6. Empty the Maggi masala tastemaker (from the packet) into the pan. Stir well.

  6. Cook until done

    7. Let it cook for 2–3 minutes. Stir occasionally. Cook until the water is mostly absorbed and the noodles are soft.

  7. Serve hot

    8. Turn off the heat. Serve immediately and enjoy your hot, spicy Maggi!
